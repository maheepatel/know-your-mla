# Know Your MLA — Public Accountability Dashboard

An interactive web-based dashboard that provides comprehensive information about Karnataka's elected Members of Legislative Assembly (MLAs). This platform promotes transparency and public accountability through data-driven insights.

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Active-brightgreen) ![Updated](https://img.shields.io/badge/Updated-May%202025-blue) ![Constituencies](https://img.shields.io/badge/Constituencies-224-orange)

---

## 📊 About This Dashboard

**Know Your MLA** is a public accountability platform designed to help citizens understand their representatives' performance, fund utilization, and accessibility. Built with transparent, verified data from multiple authoritative sources.

---

## 🎯 Key Features & Coverage

### 1. **MLA Performance Rankings**
- Comprehensive rankings of all 224 MLAs
- Multi-factor scoring system
- Top performers to bottom performers view
- Party-wise breakdown (INC, BJP, JDS, Independent)

### 2. **Constituency Development Fund Analysis**
- **Allocated vs Actual Spending**: Track fund utilization across all 224 constituencies
- District-wise fund distribution patterns
- Sector-wise impact analysis
- Visual comparison of planned vs actual expenditure

### 3. **Real Constituency Impact**
- Documented infrastructure projects completed
- Sector-wise project breakdown
- Current utilization rates
- Potential impact if full fund allocation was used

### 4. **Public Accessibility & Sentiment**
- MLA public accessibility scores
- Public sentiment tracker
- Constituent engagement metrics
- Office availability and responsiveness data

### 5. **Economic Potential Model**
- Karnataka economic growth projections
- Infrastructure development potential
- Sector-wise economic analysis
- Regional development opportunities

### 6. **MLA Profiles**
- Individual MLA detailed profiles
- Personal background and qualifications
- Electoral history
- Committee memberships

## 📚 Data Sources

This dashboard aggregates data from trusted, authoritative sources:

- **KLLADS** - Karnataka Local Level Administration Data System
- **CIVIC Bangalore** - Citizen research organization
- **B.PAC** - Budget and Policy Centre
- **ECI** - Election Commission of India
- **ADR/MyNeta** - Association for Democratic Reforms
- **Government Records** - Official Karnataka state records
- **Deccan Herald** - News and verification

*Last Updated: May 2025*

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the Dashboard**: Simply open `know-your-mla.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Search for Your MLA**: Use the search bar to find your constituency or MLA name
3. **Explore Rankings**: View full rankings and compare MLAs
4. **View Detailed Profiles**: Click on any MLA card to see comprehensive details
5. **Analyze Funds**: Check budget allocation and utilization in your constituency
6. **Track Impact**: See documented projects and their outcomes
